## 4) Data Safety Form (Required)
Prepare answers for each data type your app **collects**, **shares**, and **uses**.

**Common data types for apps like TribalMingle** (mark what applies):
- Personal info: name, email, phone
- Financial info (if payments)
- Location (precise or approximate)
- Messages / photos / user‑generated content
- Device info / analytics

For each data type, specify:
- Collected? (Yes/No)
- Shared? (Yes/No)
- Required? (Yes/No)
- Purpose (e.g., account management, app functionality, analytics)
- Data handling (encrypted in transit? deletion on request?)
